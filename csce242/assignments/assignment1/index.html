Fortnite

What is Fortnite?

Fortnite is a video game that was designed and released by Epic Games in 2017. The video game quickly took over the gaming world with its unique twists on a classic battle royale styled game. The game has generated over 26 Billion dollars in revenue to date and continues to set records year after year. The community has expanded accross multiple platforms including twitch, youtube, instagram and tiktok making Fortnite one of the biggest video games of all time.

With all of these accomplishments, Fortnite has also been in the news for other reasons. The company Epic Games agreed in a settlement with the Federal Trade Comission that they used design tricks to trick their users into making accidental purchases on their game. The company has agreed to a $245 million dollar settlement that is to be payed out to players that apply online. The 37 million users that this applies to have been notified via email by the FTC on how to recieve their refund.

Players eligble for refund

  • You were charged in-game currency for unwanted items between January 2017 and September 2022.
  • Your child charged your credit card without you knowing between January 2017 and November 2018.
  • You were locked out of your account between January 2017 and September 2022 after disputing the unauthorized charges to your credit card company.
