Welcome to NotesSimple

What is NotesSimple?

NotesSimple is an innovative new app that allows students to actively record their lectures and automatically translate them to notes. The app is designed for students at any level that have access to technology in the classroom. The app uses voice to text technology to log every word that your proffessor says and then creates a transcript of the text. After you are done recording the lecture, the app will scan the entire text file and highlight key words or phrases and separate them into sections. It will then summarize the text and highlight key information that the proffessor emphasizes. A second document will then be produced that has all of your notes for the day in one place.

How to use NotesSimple?

First, visit our download page to install the software onto your computer or mobile device. Once you have the application installed you can begin by creating an account with us. Once you do that, you can open up your device at your next lecture or meeting and hit the start button. The application will being listening to the speaker and will continue to log infomration until you hit the stop button. Afterwards depening on the version you are using, you will be able to access different features such as summarization, key points, definitions, examples, and text translations. Your information will then be stored in the app so that you can go back and access it at any time.
